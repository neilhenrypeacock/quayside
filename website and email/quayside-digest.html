Market overview
White fish · Scottish ports
Cod surges at Peterhead.
Saithe tells a different story.
A strong morning at Peterhead with cod up 12% on last week's average — volume was higher than usual, yet prices held firm, suggesting solid buyer demand from mainland processors. Haddock Grade I continues its run, now up for the third consecutive session.

Saithe is the outlier. Soft pricing across Aberdeen, Fraserburgh and Lerwick points to oversupply, likely from larger pelagic vessels landing bigger volumes than buyers can absorb. If you're flexible on species this week, saithe represents the clearest value in the market.
