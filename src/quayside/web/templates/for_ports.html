How it works
  1. 01

    Sign up — we verify you’re the port

    Fill in a short form with your port name and contact details. We’ll reach out within 24 hours to confirm you’re a legitimate operator — a quick email exchange to make sure the right people are in control of your port’s data.

    Once confirmed, you’re set up with a port account and ready to upload. No software to install, no IT involvement required.

    Free to join. Always. Sign up →
  2. 02

    Upload your prices — any format

    After the auction, forward your daily price sheet to prices@quayside.fish or upload directly via your port page. XLS, PDF, CSV — whatever you already produce. We extract, normalise, and prepare your dashboard data automatically.

    Your history builds with every upload. The longer you’re on Quayside, the richer your trend data and market comparisons become.

    Takes under two minutes once you’re set up.
  3. 03

    Review, confirm — your data goes live

    After each upload, log in to see your prices in their simplest form. Check the data is correct — one click to confirm, one click to edit if anything needs fixing. Once you confirm, your full dashboard opens and your prices are published to subscribers.

    You always have the final say before anything goes live. We learn from every correction and improve automatically over time.

Good to know

It costs you nothing.
Ever.

Quayside is funded by trade subscribers — buyers, merchants and processors who pay for the aggregated view. Ports never pay. The dashboard, the benchmarks, the marketing exposure — all free, permanently.

Other ports see
ranges, not rates.

Subscribers see market-wide benchmarks: min, average, max across all ports. Nobody sees your individual port's raw prices. When fewer than three ports report a species, no port is named at all.

If we get something
wrong, you fix it.

Every daily data pull gets a confirmation step before it goes live. One click to approve, one click to edit. You stay in control. We learn from every correction and improve automatically.
