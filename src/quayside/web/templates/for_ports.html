How it works
  1. 01

    We set you up — no work on your end

    Quayside already collects publicly available auction data from ports across the UK. When you join, we pre-populate your dashboard with the data we already hold — so you can see your market position from day one.

    We've built the extraction pipeline, the normalisation layer, and the cross-port comparison engine. All you do is confirm the data is accurate. There is no setup, no integration, and no IT involvement required.

    No signup, no software, no cost.
  2. 02

    One click to confirm, one click to edit

    Each day, your price data arrives pre-filled — extracted automatically from whatever you send us. You get a quick review link: glance at the numbers, and hit Confirm if they look right. Done. Takes ten seconds.

    If anything needs correcting, click Edit, fix the cell, and confirm. We learn from every correction and improve automatically. You always have the final say before anything goes live.

    It's quality control, not data entry.
  3. 03

    Your data goes live to subscribers

    Once confirmed, your prices are published to Quayside subscribers — fish buyers, merchants, and processors across the UK who rely on the daily digest for market intelligence.

    Your dashboard updates in real time. Your prices contribute to the most complete picture of the UK fish market available anywhere — and when you're competitive on a species, buyers see it.

Good to know

It costs you nothing.
Ever.

Quayside is funded by trade subscribers — buyers, merchants and processors who pay for the aggregated view. Ports never pay. The dashboard, the benchmarks, the marketing exposure — all free, permanently.

Other ports see
ranges, not rates.

Subscribers see market-wide benchmarks: min, average, max across all ports. Nobody sees your individual port's raw prices. When fewer than three ports report a species, no port is named at all.

If we get something
wrong, you fix it.

Every daily data pull gets a confirmation step before it goes live. One click to approve, one click to edit. You stay in control. We learn from every correction and improve automatically.
