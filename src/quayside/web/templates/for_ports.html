Why this matters to your bottom line

Your data works for you
every single day.

Every port on Quayside gets daily visibility with buyers, merchants, and processors who are actively deciding where to source — while you gain the market intelligence to act on.

Know where you
stand in the market

For every species you sell, see where your price sits in the national range — today and over time. Understand how you're trending, not just what you sold. That's market intelligence you can act on, and share with your sellers.

Buyers see when
you're competitive

Quayside trade members use the dashboard to decide where to source. Your prices are visible to a pool of serious buyers — in their dashboard throughout the day, and in their inbox each afternoon after the markets close. Daily visibility you don't have to do anything to earn.

Good for the port.
Great for your vessels.

Show sellers where their catch ranks nationally. When your monkfish is achieving top prices, that's a concrete retention argument. Vessels that know they're landing at a strong, visible market stay with you — and landing more is everything.

Good to know

It costs
you nothing.

Quayside is funded by trade subscribers — buyers, merchants and processors who pay for the aggregated view. Ports don't pay. The dashboard, the benchmarks, your own port email, the buyer visibility — all included.

Market transparency is good
for everyone.

The biggest ports in the UK already publish their prices. When potential buyers and your sellers can see the market is healthy and competitive, that's a positive for the whole industry. Other ports see the national range — min, average, max — not your individual rates. Fewer than three ports reporting a species? No port is named.
