FAIR QUESTIONS

Sharing your price data is a big decision. Here are the questions we'd ask too.

"Won't competitors use our prices against us?"

They'll see your prices — and you'll see theirs. Transparency is a two-way street. When the whole market is visible, competition happens on quality and service, not information gaps. The ports that benefit most from opaque pricing are the ones with the least to offer. If your port is competitive, visibility is your advantage.

"Do we lose control of our data?"

No. Every price record you submit goes through an approval step — we extract the data, send it back to you to review, and only publish once you confirm. If something looks wrong, you reject it. You can also withdraw from the network at any time and your historical data will be removed.

"We don't have time for another system."

There is no system. You send us whatever you already produce — a spreadsheet, a PDF, or fill in our template. The same file you'd print for the noticeboard. We handle everything from there.

"What does it cost?"

Nothing. Ports pay nothing to be on Quayside. You get the dashboard, the AI analyst, the daily digest, and the visibility — all free. Quayside is funded by paid subscriptions from fish merchants and trade buyers who use the cross-port intelligence tools.
