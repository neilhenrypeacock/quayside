Frequently asked
How does the 7-day free trial work?

Sign up with your email and you get full access to the Trade dashboard for 7 days — no payment details required. At the end of the trial, if you'd like to continue you can subscribe at £95/month. If not, your access simply ends with no charge and no obligation.

Where does the data come from?

Quayside aggregates publicly available auction data directly from UK port websites and official auction reports — including Peterhead, Brixham, Newlyn, Scrabster, and Lerwick. Data is collected every morning and normalised into a single consistent format.

Species names, grades, and price formats vary by port — we handle all of that so you see a clean, comparable view regardless of source.

Which ports are covered?

We currently have live daily data from Peterhead, Brixham, Newlyn, Scrabster, and Lerwick. We're actively expanding and adding new ports throughout 2026 — all new ports are included in your existing subscription at no extra cost.

How accurate is the data?

All data is sourced directly from official port publications and auction reports. We run automated quality checks on every data pull — flagging anomalies, unexpected gaps, and price swings that look like errors. Port operators can also review and confirm their own data before it's published.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes, absolutely. There's no minimum term. Cancel any time from your account settings and you won't be charged again. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Is there an annual plan?

We're working on an annual plan with a discounted rate — coming soon. If you'd like to be notified when it's available, drop us a line at hello@quayside.fish.
