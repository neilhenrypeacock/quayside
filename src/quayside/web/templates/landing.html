{# ─── Section 2: The Problem ─── #}
THE PROBLEM

Most fish merchants buy from the port they know, pay whatever the price is that day, and have no way to tell if they got a good deal or a bad one. Prices vary significantly across the UK — but that information is scattered across half a dozen port websites, PDF sheets, and phone calls to contacts at the docks. Some ports don't publish at all. The ones with the best networks have the advantage. Everyone else flies blind.

{# ─── Section 3: One Platform, Three Tools ─── #}
ONE PLATFORM, THREE TOOLS

Quayside collects auction prices from ports across the UK and delivers them to buyers every day. One place to see what fish is selling for, everywhere. The information comes to you. You don't have to chase it.

Daily Email Digest
The morning briefing

Prices, movers, and cross-port spreads from every port we cover — delivered to your inbox before the day's buying begins. One email. The whole market.

Port Dashboards
Deep dive by port

See how a port did today. How it's trended over 90 days. How it's priced against every other port in the network — so you know whether you're seeing a fair read of the market, or an outlier.

Trade Intelligence
Every species. Every port. One view.

See where haddock is cheapest this morning. Track how monkfish has moved over the past month. Compare ports side by side. Make buying decisions with the whole market in front of you — not just your corner of it.
