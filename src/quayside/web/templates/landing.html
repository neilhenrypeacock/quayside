{# ─── Section 2: The Problem ─── #}
THE PROBLEM

Fish auction prices in the UK are scattered across dozens of port websites, published in different formats, at different times — or in many cases, not published online at all. There is no single source of truth for what fish sold for this morning. Quayside changes that.

{# ─── Section 3: One Platform, Three Tools ─── #}
ONE PLATFORM, THREE TOOLS

Quayside collects auction prices from a growing network of UK and Irish ports, and turns fragmented data into clear, usable intelligence.

Daily Digest
The morning briefing

Prices, movers, and cross-port spreads from every port we cover. Delivered to your inbox each trading day.

Port Dashboards
Deep dive by port

90-day trends, grade breakdowns, and market positioning — see how any port is performing against itself and the wider market.

Trade Intelligence
The full picture

Species-first view across all ports. Best prices, widest spreads, and how today compares to the last 30 days.
