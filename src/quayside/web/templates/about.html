Quayside

Every UK fish auction,
in one place.

Fish market data in the UK has always been fragmented — each port publishing prices in a different format, on a different schedule, using different species names. Quayside brings it together into a single, daily-updated feed that works for buyers, sellers, and ports alike.

How we get the data

We collect publicly available auction data from each port's own website — automatically, every morning. But we're also building direct data partnerships with ports so the feed gets faster, more complete, and officially endorsed over time.

Today, five ports feed into Quayside. More join regularly — either through our automated pipeline or by sending data directly.
